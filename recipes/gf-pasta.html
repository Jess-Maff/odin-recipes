GF Pasta

Description

This recipe will allow you to make perfectly cooked gluten free pasta everytime without fail.

No more disintigrating or chewy noodles in your gluten free lifestyle!

Ingredients

  • 1 box Gluten Free Pasta
  • 2 quarts water
  • a pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Bring water and pinch of salt to a boil in appropriate sized pot

    2. (To get boiling faster cover pot)

  2. Once boiling add gluten free pasta to the boiling water and set a timer for 2 minutes less than shortest time on pasta packaging
  3. When timer goes off drain pasta Do Not rinse pasta
  4. Serve pasta with sauce of choice
