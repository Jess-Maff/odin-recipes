Adult Lunchables

Description

Simple lunch to pack for youself.

Ingredients

  • Cheese(s) of your choice
  • Lunchmeat(s) of your choice

    • Optional

  • Fruits
  • Veggies

Steps

  • Cut all ingredients to bite sized pieces
  • Neatly pack them all into a container in an aesthetically pleasing way
  • Enjoy all at once or snack throughout the day
