Grilled Cheese

Description

The ultimate easiest comfort food. You will need a grilled cheese maker that costs $8.

Ingredients

  • Bread (at least 2 slices)
  • Cheese (american melty slices work best)

Steps

  1. Plug in the grilled cheese maker and allow to warm up
  2. Place 1 to 2 slices of cheese between 2 slices of bread
  3. Place the cold cheese sandwich in the grilled cheese maker
  4. When indicator on your device goes off open the grilled cheese maker
  5. Carefully place sandwich on plate (or papertowel to avoid dishes)
  6. Enjoy alone or paired with your favorite soup
